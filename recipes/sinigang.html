Sinigang

Sour Pork and Vegetable Stew - A Filipino Classic

It's simple to cook some sinigang for any occasion! Gather the core ingredients: pork belly, roma tomatoes, onions, kangkong leaves, string beans and a packet of Tamarind Soup Mix. Prepare the ingredients: cube the pork belly; quarter the tomatoes; slice and onions; pluck the leaf bunches from the base of the stem on the kangkong leaves; and cut the string beans to a shorter length.

In a large pot, heat up some cooking oil. Sauté the onions and cook the pork belly cubes until lightly browned. Pour in some water and bring to a boil. Add in the tomatoes then simmer until the protein is tender. Stir in packet mix and string beans. After a few minutes, turn off the heat, put in the kangkong leaves and cover. Once the leaves have cooked and softened, serve with a bowl of rice. Enjoy!

Steps:

  1. Using a large pot, heat up the cooking oil.
  2. Sauté the onion until the layers separate.
  3. Add the pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Pour the water and bring to a boil. Remove scum if desired.
  5. Add tomatoes and simmer until pork is tender.
  6. Mix in the sinigang packet and string beans. Simmer for another 8 minutes.
  7. Turn off heat, put in the kangkong leaves and cover.
  8. Serve when leaves are cooked. Eat with rice and enjoy!
